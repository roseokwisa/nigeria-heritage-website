Welcome to Nigeria's Heritage



Welcome to Nigerian Heritage Education, a dedicated platform for teachers striving to create culturally inclusive and engaging learning environments for students in Classes 10 through 12.

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality resources that celebrate Nigeria’s rich history and cultural diversity. Here, you’ll find books, videos, articles, and interactive tools to help you integrate Nigerian heritage into your curriculum. Together, let’s inspire the next generation to appreciate and preserve their cultural identity.





Explore our resources, share your experiences, and join us in fostering a deeper understanding of Nigeria’s past and present.

About

Chisom Egbulonu is a master’s student in Educational Leadership at Vancouver Island University, currently in her third semester and working on a project focused on integrating Nigerian history and culture into education. Originally from Imo State, Nigeria, and having lived in the UAE for over eight years, she holds a bachelor’s degree in Microbiology but pursued a diploma in education due to her passion for teaching and working with children. Chisom’s deep appreciation for history and culture has shaped her teaching approach, emphasizing understanding students’ backgrounds and identities. Her project aims to address the lack of adequate teaching resources by creating a website that offers interactive tools, articles, books, and videos to help teachers incorporate Nigerian heritage into their lessons. The platform will also enable educators to share ideas and resources, fostering a deeper connection to cultural identity and promoting respect for Nigeria’s rich history.


Our Motivation:

Growing up in Nigeria, we witnessed firsthand, the lack of accessible resources for teaching Nigerian history and culture in schools.

This inspired us to create a platform where teachers can find curated materials to enrich their classrooms.

Our goal is to empower educators to foster cultural pride and awareness among students.


Join Us:

We invite you to connect with us and share your feedback. Together, we can make cultural education more impactful and meaningful.
